Day in the Life

What the
Work Actually Looks Like

Vacant Lot Maintenance

Out Early.
Routes Ready. Lots Done.

  • Depart yard by 7 AM with a pre-loaded route for your ward assignment
  • Operate zero-turn or autonomous mowers on lots ranging from 5,000 sq ft to over an acre
  • Trim perimeters, clear debris, and log each completed lot in the digital tracking system
  • Coordinate with crew leader on routing adjustments, flagged properties, and 311 requests
  • Return equipment, record production totals, and brief for the next day's schedule
Urban Forestry

In the Trees.
In the Community.

  • Morning briefing covers storm damage reports, scheduled pruning blocks, and planting deployments
  • Bucket truck or climbing operations — prune, remove, or treat as assigned by the certified arborist lead
  • Interact with residents and ward staff when working in neighborhoods
  • Document tree health in the city's forestry inventory system after each work order
  • Planting seasons bring full-crew days in the ground — soil prep, staking, and mulching

How to Apply

The Hiring
Process

1
Submit Online

Complete the City of Cleveland civil service application at careers.clevelandohio.gov. You'll need a valid ID and employment history. Takes about 15 minutes.

2
Civil Service Exam

Most positions require a written or skills-based civil service exam. Exams are scheduled regularly — you'll receive a date by email after your application is reviewed.

3
Panel Interview

Qualified candidates are invited to a structured interview with Public Works supervisors. No experience with government hiring? We'll walk you through it.

4
Background & Physical

Standard background check and occupational health screening. CDL holders also complete a DOT physical. Results typically returned within 5–10 business days.

5
Start Date

Receive your offer letter, complete onboarding paperwork, and show up ready to work. Most new hires start within 3–4 weeks of a conditional offer.
